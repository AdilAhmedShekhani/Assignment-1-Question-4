Karachi City Of Lights

Karachi is the capital of the Pakistani province of Sindh.
Situated on the Arabian Sea, Karachi serves as a transport hub, and is home to
Pakistan's two largest seaports, the Port of Karachi and Port Bin Qasim, as well as the busiest airport in Pakistan.
